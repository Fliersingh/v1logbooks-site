Features

Everything a pilot needs, nothing they don't

Built from the ground up for Indian aviation regulations and real operational workflows.

Direct eGCA upload

Upload flights straight to the DGCA eGCA portal from your phone. No manual data entry, no double-handling. Your authenticated session, your data.

Offline-first

Log flights without a connection. Everything syncs automatically when you're back online. Your logbook is always available, wherever you fly.

FDTL tracking

Automatic duty time calculations per CAR Section 7. Know your 7-day, 14-day, 28-day, and 90-day totals at a glance.

Approval tracking

CPL & ATPL syllabus

Recency & currency

How it works

Three steps from logbook to DGCA

01

Log your flight

Enter flight details in seconds. Aircraft, route, departure and arrival times, exercise type, and remarks. Works offline — syncs when you're connected.

02

Upload to eGCA

One tap to push your flight directly to the DGCA eGCA portal. No copy-paste, no browser juggling. The app handles the authenticated session for you.

03

Track approval

Monitor pending, approved, and rejected flights from the logbook view. When your verifier acts, the status updates automatically.

DGCA compliance

Built to Indian aviation standards

Every feature is built against official DGCA regulations — not interpretations of them.

CAR Section 7 FDTL

Duty time limits per CAR Section 7 Series J Part III (Rev 2, 26 Mar 2024). 35h/7d, 65h/14d, 100h/28d, 300h/90d.

eGCA portal integration

Flights submitted via the official DGCA eGCA portal with your own authenticated credentials. No third-party intermediaries.

Verifier workflow

DGCA-mandated verifier assignment on every submitted flight. CFI, airline verifier, and self-attestation roles supported.

CPL/ATPL syllabus tracking

Full DGCA CPL syllabus exercise log with progress tracking. Covers all required exercises across the training framework.

Medical & licence validity

Track Class 1 and Class 2 medical validity periods. Alerts before expiry so you're never caught off-guard.

Simulator session logging

Log simulator sessions separately from flight time. Correct classification for DGCA reporting and currency requirements.
